ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key features of our company

Background

Our products are made for and by a family of pet lovers. And they're more than just everyday pet supplies. We offer many dog friendly products such as crates, potty pads, potty bags, and treats but one of our best sellers is our lightweight dog raincoat. We provide practical solutions for today's devoted pet parents. Our entire Frisco line is available exclusively at Chewy.com! .

Passion

We stand for safe cruelty free products for your precious pups. Unlike other companies, we care about you and your pets safety and we will do anything to achieve a safe environment .

Design

All of our products are designed and made with care and passion. We will never release a product if we are unsure of the design or the quality. We offer a couple types of our dog raincoats such as transparent, solid colors, and even cool designs that are made to suit your dogs personality.

Costumer Support

Here at Frisk, we try our very best to make our customers satisfied and happy. If there is a problem with any of your purchases or our products, please contact us at friskproducts@gmail.com. Have questions? Ask. Have concerns? Report them. Your happiness is always our first concern.

We know design.

Our top notch dog rain coats not only keep your favorite furry friends clean, it also keeps your house clean and floors shiny!
